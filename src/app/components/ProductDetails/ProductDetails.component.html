The Napoleon STARfire™ 35 Gas Fireplace is the perfect statement piece for your home. Advanced burner technology provides a high, centralized flame that creates a glowing ambiance that is perfect for your living room or great room. With many decorator options like a modern fire cradle with a Topaz ember bed, or a hand painted, realistic, PHAZER® log set, your room will reflect your personality perfectly. You can even customize the STARfire™ 35 further with the addition of enhancement kits, decorative surrounds and MIRRO-FLAME™ or brick panels. Enjoy the warm glow of your fireplace without the fire with our exclusive NIGHT LIGHT™. The STARfire™ 35 Gas Fireplace is available in natural gas and propane, and meets the new safety standards with the included Safety Screen.
